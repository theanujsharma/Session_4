Profile

Designer

This is me A UI Artist and Graphic Designer based in London who has a degree in Graphic Design and has 15 years of work experience, including experience in print and digital design. The previous projects have included work in/for: Brand Identity, Logo Design, Packaging Design, Advertising Design, Print & Digital Marketing, Web Design (Wix, Shopify), UI/UX Design, Social Media Management, Seo, and Google Ads. Visual identities and surprising design solutions are created for a wide range of clients with different commercial backgrounds such as advertising, editorial, entertainment, film, gastronomy, music, and fashion. Helping teams turn their ideas into a living brand.
